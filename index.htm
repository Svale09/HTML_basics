Hatake Kakashi

Early life

Hatake Kakashi was born as a son of a great and respected Konoha Shinobi known as the White Fang.
He loved his father and he looked up to him, wanting to become a grat shinobi like his father
One day a tragedy happened, his father sacrificed the mission in order to save his comrades. Even though he saved lives of manz of his shinobi comrades the village frowned upon him. The White Fang couldn't handle the embarassment and killed himself.

ANBU carrer

After his fathers death Kakashi decided to follow all of the rules. He washed his house every day, he knew how to cook very well, all in all he was very disciplined.
That discipline eventually led to his recognition by his sensei and the Hokage at the time: Minato Namikaze. Minato decided to put him in the ANBU to heal his heart of the sorrow he felt. But what he didn't know is that the time itself is the only thing that can heal a broken heart.

Sensei

After exiting from the ANBU, Kakashi became a sensei and started teaching and leading young gening ninjas. He tried to show them the importance of teamwork but many of them failed his tests. Until one team, team 7.
